## [0.1.0] - 2026-04-28

Initial public release. Pick a screen region, record it at 60 FPS with mic and system audio, drag the timeline back while paused to re-record from that point, trim with Mark In / Mark Out, preview, and export to MP4. Autosaves while recording.
